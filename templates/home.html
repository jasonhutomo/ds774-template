Welcome

Welcome to our website! We are a clothing brand that was started in January 2022. Our founder, Jason Hutomo, is a student at the University of New Hampshire. He is currently a junior and is majoring in information systems and business analytics. Our company was founded in hopes of producing affordable and stylish clothes for people of all shapes and sizes. Our product is targeted towards teenagers and young adults. Our products are made with high quality materials. Our brand prioritizes using environmentally sustainable practices. We work closely with our textile suppliers to make this possible. We are solely an e-business and conduct all of our sales through our website. We don't have a retail location at the moment, but we would like to have a brick and mortar shop in the next couple of years. We are working with our partners and stakeholders to work on a plan for a retail location. More news will come in the following months.

We offer a variety of clothes including t-shirts, pants, shorts, and hoodies. We would like to produce more types of clothes. We are working to put out our next line of clothes. This new line will include products that we haven't released in the past. Some new items coming include socks, boxers, windbreakers, and mesh shorts. We are planning on dropping this new capsule at the beginning of Summer 2022. We will also be having our very first community pop-up shop event to celebrate this drop. Stay tuned to our social media pages to find out the details of this event! We thank you for your continuous support and we look forward to seeing more of you wearing our apparel!
